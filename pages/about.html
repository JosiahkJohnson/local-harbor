Our Story:

Local harbor had its humble beginnings in Memphis Tennessee. The first retail store begain in a foreclosure auction for a warehouse full of random freight. Local harbor had all of the infrastructure needed to begin its local shipping business.

A later expansion gave the company access to the Mississippi river to allow for shipments potentially reaching around the world. Orders were taken by mail and were fulfilled within a week. What started with local shoppers expanded world-wide.

Recently, localharbor launched its own website to have direct control over its shipping services. Previously, local harbor was reliant on third-party retailers for their online presence.
